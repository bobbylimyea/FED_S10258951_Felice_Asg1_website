Mental Health

What is mental health?

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It has a great impacton how we think, feel, and act. It helps us to determine how we manage our stress and make the right choices in life like, do we want to live a healthy lifestyle? etc. (Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration, 2023).

Why is mental health so important?

Prioritize mental well-being, as it plays a crucial role in overall health and functioning. With a good mental-being, you are then able to handle the differents things on your own plate better than you always do and it makes a huge difference.

What are some symptoms of not having a good mental well-being?

  • Sleeping too much or too little
  • Always feeling really tired and not wanting to do anything
  • Not having much appetite
  • Isolating by yourself more often than usual
  • Having thoughts of self-harm
  • Easily irritated or upset with your peers or family

How can we maintain good mental well-being?

  1. Pick up a hobby
    2. Find activities that you enjoy and do them periodically to relieve stress (e.g sports, crochet, reading)
  2. Practice journaling
    3. Take some time in the day to pen down things you are grateful for, or what you are looking forward to in the future
  3. Set goals or targets for the day
    4. This will give you something to look forward to and provide a sense of accomplishment
